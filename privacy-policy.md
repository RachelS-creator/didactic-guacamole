# Privacy Policy for Aluna Flow

Effective date: November 21, 2025  
Owner: Rachel Sturm

Aluna Flow (“the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy.  
This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Information You Provide  
• Account information such as email (if provided).  
• Health-related cycle tracking information that you manually enter into the app.  
• Any data directly entered into the app’s features.

### b. Automatically Collected Information  
• Device information such as device model and OS version.  
• Purchase and subscription status (via Apple’s App Store APIs).  
• Crash logs and anonymized analytics (if enabled).

We **do NOT** sell or share personal data with third parties.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
• Provide app functionality  
• Maintain premium subscription access  
• Improve app performance  
• Restore purchases  
• Provide customer support

All health-related data stays **only on your device** unless you choose to share it.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

Aluna Flow uses:
• **RevenueCat** (purchase management)  
• **Apple App Store** (subscription processing)

These services do not receive your personal data beyond what is necessary for subscription validation.

---

## 4. Data Retention
Data stored on your device remains until you delete the app.  
Subscription records are retained only as required for fraud prevention and App Store policies.

---

## 5. Your Rights
You may:
• Delete your data by deleting the app  
• Cancel subscriptions via your Apple ID  
• Request support at: **rachel.sturm@icloud.com**
